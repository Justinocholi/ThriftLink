Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save a vendor to my favorites?
When browsing vendors, simply click the "Save" or heart icon on any vendor's profile. You can view all your saved vendors in the "Saved Vendors" section of your dashboard.
How do I contact a vendor?
Each verified vendor has a WhatsApp button on their profile. Click it to start a conversation directly on WhatsApp. Make sure you have WhatsApp installed on your device.
What if I have an issue with a vendor?
If you experience any problems with a vendor, you can report them using the "Report a Vendor" option above. Our team will investigate and take appropriate action to ensure platform safety.
How are vendors verified on ThriftLink?
All vendors go through a thorough verification process including identity verification, business legitimacy checks, and customer review analysis. Only trusted vendors receive our verification badge.
Can I track my orders?
Yes! Visit the "My Orders" section in your dashboard to view all your order history and current order statuses. You can also contact vendors directly via WhatsApp for real-time updates.
Is my personal information secure?
Absolutely. We take data security seriously and use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal information. We never share your data with third parties without your consent.
