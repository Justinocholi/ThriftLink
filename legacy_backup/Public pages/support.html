Frequently Asked Questions

What is Thrift Link? ‚ñº
Thrift Link is Nigeria's largest WhatsApp vendor marketplace that connects buyers with verified sellers across the country. We help you discover trusted vendors, chat directly via WhatsApp, and shop safely without any middleman fees.
Is Thrift Link free to use? ‚ñº
Yes, browsing vendors and connecting via WhatsApp is completely free for buyers. Sellers pay a small fee only when they complete transactions through our platform.
How do I know if a vendor is trustworthy? ‚ñº
Look for verified badges, check ratings and reviews, see their response time, and review their order history. All our vendors go through strict verification including ID checks and business validation.
What if I have a problem with my order? ‚ñº
First, try resolving the issue directly with the vendor. If that doesn't work, contact our support team with your order details and chat records. We'll help mediate and find a fair solution.
How do I find vendors in my area? ‚ñº
Use our location filter when browsing categories or searching. You can set your preferred delivery radius and see vendors who can serve your location.
What payment methods are accepted? ‚ñº
Payment is arranged directly with vendors. Most accept bank transfers, mobile money (PalmPay, Opay), and cash on delivery. Always use traceable payment methods for your security.
Can I return items I'm not satisfied with? ‚ñº
Return policies vary by vendor. Always discuss return conditions before purchasing. Most verified vendors offer reasonable return policies for defective or misrepresented items.
How do I become a verified vendor? ‚ñº
Submit your application with valid ID, business registration (if applicable), bank account details, and product samples. Our verification process takes 24-48 hours and includes background checks.
What are the fees for selling on Thrift Link? ‚ñº
Basic listing is free. We charge a small transaction fee (2-5%) only when you complete sales through our platform. Premium features like featured listings have additional costs.
How do I improve my vendor rating? ‚ñº
Respond quickly to messages, provide accurate product descriptions, deliver on time, offer excellent customer service, and encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews.
How do I report suspicious vendors? ‚ñº
Use the report button on vendor profiles, contact our support team immediately, or send details to our safety hotline. Include screenshots of suspicious conversations and any evidence.
What should I do if I'm asked to pay upfront? ‚ñº
Be very cautious with upfront payments. Only pay in advance for established vendors with excellent ratings. For new vendors, prefer cash on delivery or secure payment methods.
How do I ensure safe meetups for pickup? ‚ñº
Meet in public places during daylight hours, bring a friend if possible, verify the vendor's identity, inspect items before paying, and trust your instincts if something feels wrong.
